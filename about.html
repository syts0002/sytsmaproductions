About the artist

Sean Sytsma is an Ottawa-based photographer, videographer and musician. Growing up on a cattle farm in Athens, Ontario, Sytsma is fascinated by landscape and the environmental and economic concerns that face contemporary farmers.

For his recent photo series, Pastures, Sytsma spent four months in the summer of 2018 following his father’s herd of approximately 250 cow and calf pairs as they rotational grazed an area of over 1200 acres. Sytsma has attempted to capture each of the animal’s unique personalities, with some photos showing the baby caves just hours after being born.

With the current trend of farm to table dinning, Sytsma hopes to showcase the beauty and poetry of our agricultural environment and demonstrate that food is not just something bought in a store.
